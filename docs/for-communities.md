# PRUEBA — For Communities

## What is PRUEBA?

PRUEBA helps your community keep a permanent record of everything you do together — your sessions, your decisions, your story.

Think of it like a community diary that can never be lost, edited, or deleted. Every record is saved safely and can be verified by anyone.

## How It Works

1. **You do your thing.** Run a football session, hold a community meeting, make a decision together.

2. **Report it.** Tell your community bot what happened — how many people came, what you did, what was decided.

3. **It's recorded.** The details are saved permanently. A copy goes to a safe storage system (IPFS) and a receipt is created on a public ledger (Base).

4. **Anyone can verify it.** The record is public and tamper-proof. Funders, partners, or community members can check it anytime.

## Why This Matters

- **Proof of participation**: Show funders and partners exactly what your community does, with verifiable records
- **Community memory**: Build a permanent history of your organization's activities
- **Transparent governance**: Every decision is recorded — who voted, what was decided, when
- **No gatekeepers**: Your records belong to your community, not a platform

## What Gets Recorded

### Sessions
- Who organized it
- Where it happened
- How many people participated
- What type of activity (training, match, workshop, etc.)
- Any notes from the organizer

### Decisions
- What was proposed
- How people voted
- What was decided
- Who was present

## Getting Started

To use PRUEBA for your community:

1. **Talk to us.** We'll help set up a bot for your community (Telegram, WhatsApp, or other platforms).
2. **Start recording.** After each session or meeting, report it through the bot.
3. **Watch your history grow.** Visit the dashboard to see your community's record over time.

## FAQ

**Is this blockchain?**
Yes, but you don't need to understand blockchain to use it. The bot handles everything. You just talk to it like a person.

**Does it cost money?**
Recording is free for communities. The protocol handles the small transaction fees.

**Can records be deleted?**
No. That's the point — your community's history is permanent and can't be tampered with.

**What languages does the bot speak?**
The bot mirrors whatever language you write in. Somali, English, Spanish, Arabic — any language works.

**Who can see our records?**
Records are public by default. This is intentional — it builds trust with funders and partners. If you need private records, talk to us about options.
